About Me

I am not a graduate of computer science or anything close to that. So you may wonder how I came to be a full-stack developer! Well, it all began from a place of curiosity. I have always had keen eyes for design and I’ve always been drawn to websites with interactive features. I was often fascinated about how the internet and all its components work. Then I discovered that websites and webpages did not magically appear on the internet, they were built by actual people like myself! So, I decided to learn how to build this magical thing called a website!

This decision marked the beginning of my journey to becoming a full-stack developer. Today, I design and build websites and mobile apps that solve problems and create opportunities for people in the society. I take pride in my work and I urge you to explore my portfolio
